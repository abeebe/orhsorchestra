## ORHS Orchestra 2022-2023 Concert Season Donations

Every dollar raised is solely used for the benefit of the ORHS Orchestra program.

### Bronze Baton — $25
Name listed on Orchestra program throughout the entire year.

### Silver Baton — $50
Name listed in Orchestra Program throughout the entire year. Two free tickets to our September 2022 concert.

### Gold Baton Business Donation — $150
Business Full Color Ad (3"x3") throughout the entire year. Two free tickets to our 2022-23 Season concerts.

### Platinum Baton Business Donation — $250
Half Page Full Color Ad program throughout the entire year. Two free tickets to our 2022-23 Season concerts. Logo and link to business on our website.

### Maestro's Circle Business Donation — $500
- Full Page Full Color Ad
- Two free tickets to our 2022-23 Season concerts
- Logo and link to business on our website
- Verbal "Thank you" at all Orchestra concert programs

### Wildcat Strings Sponsorship
This donation goes directly to paying for new strings.

Approximate costs for strings:
- Violin: $20
- Viola: $30
- Cello: $65
- Bass: $100
